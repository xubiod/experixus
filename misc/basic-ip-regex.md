*Regex:*
`[0-2]?([0-9]?){2}\.[0-2]?([0-9]?){2}\.[0-2]?([0-9]?){2}\.[0-2]?([0-9]?){2}`

If given:
```
69.192.45.5
1.1.1.1
192.168.1.0
24.59.220.88
255.255.255.256
192.168.1.1.128
```

the pattern finds:
```
69.192.45.5     at 0
1.1.1.1         at 12
192.168.1.0     at 20
24.59.220.88    at 32
255.255.255.256 at 45
192.168.1.1     at 62
```
